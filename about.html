Text Wrapping Example

This paragraph demonstrates how text wraps around a floated image. On desktop, the image is floated to the left and takes 50% width. On mobile (max-width 768px), the image becomes full width and the text stacks below.

This is useful for blog posts where you want an image next to the introduction text.
